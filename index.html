Podcast hosts reveal their personal stories, talk about their journey, and share their tips for applicants.

EPISODES

#1 Our Admission History in Astana IT University

Welcome to La Vida Loca Podcast! In the first issue, we will discuss our history of admission to the AITU, talk about the romanticization of student life, and much more. Thank you for listening to us!

#2 What is the ideal student?//Mental breakdown

In this issue, we talked about the "ideal" student, and how this affects the mental state. We also shared tips that help us cope with burnout.Thank you for listening to us!

#3 How to deal with binge-eating disorder?//Eating disorder issues

In this podcast, we will dive into the topic of binge-eating disorder and discuss how to identify the signs, symptoms, and triggers of the disorder, as well as provide helpful tips and strategies for managing and overcoming it.

Join us to learn more about this difficult issue and how to find hope and healing

#4 Strict "standards" of beauty//ASIA vs EUROPE

This podcast is an exploration of the differences between beauty standards in Asia and Europe. We will discuss how these standards have changed over time, and how they differ in areas.

Join us as we explore these topics and more!
